Zelda Sample by Claire T.

Created at USC as a one-week project. I do not own any of the assets. This was a project practicing CSV level loading and enemy AI using A* pathfinding.

Game instructions: Use WASD to move and space to swing your sword. When you hit certain bushes, the soldiers will change their pathing because they continuously calculate it. This is a sample with only one level.
